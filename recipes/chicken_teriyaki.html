Chicken Teriyaki Recipe

Chicken Teriyaki is a "must know" recipe for every human -albert teriyakistein

Chicken teriyaki is a juicy and refreshing meal to ease your sweet buds, yet provide important health benefits

It consists of the tender and amazing chicken breast and the sweet and savouring sauce that is teriyaki sauce

Chicken teriyaki is delicious and fulfilling in all aspects and doesn't lose it's flavor even after 2/3 days

That is why it is really good for the whole family!

Ingredients

The following ingredients are for a family of 4's dinner

  • Chicken breasts - 4
  • Teriyaki sauce - 1.5 cups
Optional:
  • Cut green onion for aesthetic
  • Some sesame seeds for texture
  • Rice, as a carb side dish

How to make

  1. Chop up the chicken breasts into small cubes

  2. Throw them in the pan with some butter to cook

  3. Wait until they are fully white, throw some teriyaki sauce in there

  4. Wait until they look beautifully colored and then take out

  5. Serve with whatever you want, and enjoy!
